LOSS OF BIODIVERSITY

Biodiversity loss refers to the decline or disappearance of biological diversity, or the variety of living things that inhabit the planet, its different levels of biological organization, and their respective genetic variability, as well as the natural patterns present in ecosystems.


CAUSES OF BIODIVERSITY LOSS

1.Climate Change

2.Poullution

3.Destruction of habitats

4.Overexploitation of the natural environment
